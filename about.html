Welcome to TFL - The Ultimate Competitive Touch Football League

Are you ready to experience one of the most competitive and thrilling touch football leagues around? TFL is a powerful league built on the spirit of the game, where some of the best players come together to showcase skill, strength, and sportsmanship.

What TFL Offers:

  • Top Talent and Elite Competition: Join a league filled with passionate and talented players who bring their best to every match.
  • A Supportive Community with Round-the-Clock Staff: Our dedicated team is always here to keep TFL running smoothly.
  • Exciting Streams and Events: TFL shares match streams, highlights, and special events to keep everyone engaged and inspired.

Join the TFL Team – We’re Looking for You!

TFL is growing, and we’re looking for mature, committed individuals to join our team in roles like Staff, Streamers, Commentators, Referees, Admins, and Moderators. If you’re ready to contribute to something exciting and impactful, we’d love to have you join the TFL family.

Community Values and Rules:

  • No NSFW content, sexism, offensive language, or disrespectful behavior
  • Respectful chat policies and compliance with Discord’s Terms of Service
  • Zero-tolerance policy for harassment and inappropriate behavior
